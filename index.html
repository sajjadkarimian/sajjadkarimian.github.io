I work on healthcare data, trustworthy AI, and socio-technical systems, with a focus on improving data transparency, quality, and reliability in clinical environments.

About Me

I am a healthcare data researcher with a background in Computer Science and Medical Informatics. My research focuses on data transparency, trustworthy AI, and socio-technical systems in healthcare.

I am particularly interested in how data flows across clinical, technical, and organisational boundaries, and how better modelling of these flows can support safer and more accountable data-driven healthcare systems.
