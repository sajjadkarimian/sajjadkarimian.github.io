About Me

Sajjad Karimian is a researcher working at the intersection of healthcare data, trustworthy AI, and socio-technical systems. His work focuses on improving data transparency, quality, and reliability in clinical environments where data directly shapes patient safety and decision-making.

With a background in Computer Science and Medical Informatics, he applies data analytics, FAIR principles, and ontology engineering (RDF/OWL) to model and evaluate data-driven systems. He has contributed to journal and conference publications and brings additional experience in teaching and healthcare IT.
