About Me

I am a healthcare data researcher with a background in Computer Science and Medical Informatics. My work focuses on improving data transparency, quality, and reliability in healthcare systems where data directly shapes patient safety, reporting, and decision-making.

I combine data analytics, FAIR data principles, ontology engineering, and human factors methods to understand how data flows across clinical, technical, and organisational boundaries. My work supports the design of more transparent, accountable, and human-centred data-driven healthcare systems.
